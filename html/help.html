Fighters

support us more so that we can save more and more trees.!

1.Plant more trees

Although planting trees is a popular Earth Day activity, fall is the season to plant trees and shrubs. Do your homework to make sure you pick the right tree for your space.

2. Use paper wisely.

Save trees from being cut down by using less paper.Play and create with trash.Borrow, share and donate books.

3.Donation

Donate as much as you can in a nonprofit working to protect forests.

4.Local ordinances regarding tree protection.

Every town and city has laws dictating which tree species need to be protected .Knowing the laws in your area will help you be a better advocate for the trees .

5.Become a volunteer

Get involved when you see a tree coming down.Do research to find out if the tree is being cut down legally.

6.Trees and future

Make everyone aware that there is no future without trees and plants.Lack of trees will lead to destruction of our upcoming generations.
